Artificial Intelligence

Overview

A machine is considered to have artificial intelligence if it can perform taks that humans can and a human evluator can not tell the diffracne between a human and said machine.

Types of AI

AI has a wide range of applications across various industries some types of Ai are

  • Machine Learning a type of AI that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.
  • Computer Vision a type of AI that enables machines to interpret and understand visual information from the world.
  • Natural Language Processing a type of AI that enables machines to understand and generate human-like text.
  • Document Intelligence AI that extracts and analyzes information from documents.
  • Knowledge mining a type of AI that discovers and extracts hidden patterns and insights from large datasets.
  • Generative AI AI that generates new content like text, images, or audio.

Importance of AI

The world is increasingly dependent on AI technologies. AI is transforming industries by automating tasks, improving decision-making, and enabling new capabilities that were previously impossible.
